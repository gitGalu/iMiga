iMiga needs to be launched from the iOS/iPadOS Home screen:


  1. Tap the Share button in the Safari menu bar.
  2. Tap the "Add to Home Screen" option.
  3. Launch the iMiga app from your Home screen.

iMiga needs a valid Kickstart ROM file.

Imported files will be stored locally on your device.
